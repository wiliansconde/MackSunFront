POEMAS

Solar radio telescope operating at millimeter wavelengths, providing high temporal resolution observations for solar flare and astronomical time series research.

  • Frequencies: 45 / 90 GHz
  • Type: Solar radio time series
  • Resolution: up to 10ms
  • Available periods: 2011 (Nov–Dec); 2012 (Jan–Dec); 2013 (Jan–Dec); 2019 (Jun–Jul, Sep–Dec); 2020 (Jan–Mar, Sep–Nov); 2024 (Apr–Nov)
  • Records: billions
  • Location: CASLEO, Argentina
  • Note: Data coverage is not continuous due to instrument maintenance and operational downtime.

SST

Solar submillimeter telescope used for high-frequency solar observations, supporting studies of energetic processes and particle acceleration during solar flares.

  • Frequencies: 212 / 405 GHz
  • Type: Solar submillimeter monitoring
  • Resolution: 5ms, 40ms and 1s products
  • Location: CASLEO, Argentina
