Available Instruments


POEMAS

Solar radio telescope operating at millimeter wavelengths, designed for high temporal resolution observations of solar flares and fast transient events.

Frequencies: 45 / 90 GHz
Type: Solar radio time series
Resolution: 10ms
Available periods: 2011 (Nov–Dec);
2012 and 2013 (Jan–Dec);
2019 (Jun–Jul, Sep–Dec);
2020 (Jan–Mar, Sep–Nov);
2024 (Apr–Nov)
Note: Data coverage is not continuous due to instrument maintenance and operational downtime.
Location: CASLEO, Argentina

SST

Solar submillimeter telescope used for high-frequency solar observations, supporting studies of energetic processes and particle acceleration.

Frequencies: 212 / 405 GHz
Type: Solar submillimeter monitoring
Resolution: 5ms, 40ms and 1s products
Available periods: Available through the MackSun data portal according to product availability.
Note: Data coverage may vary according to instrument operation, calibration routines and product availability.
Location: CASLEO, Argentina
