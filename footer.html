MackSun is supported by CRAAM, Mackenzie Presbyterian University, CAPES and LINEA.

MackSun v2 provides solar data, including solar time series data, solar flare data and solar radio data from instruments such as POEMAS and SST. Data collected from observational instruments located in South America.
